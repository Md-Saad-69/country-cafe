Our Story

A Local Gem in
Guwahati's Heart

Coffee Country was born from a simple belief — great coffee doesn't have to be expensive, and a great café doesn't need to be fancy. Nestled along Katahbari Main Road, Garchuk, we've become the go-to spot for Guwahati's students and locals.

Whether you're catching up with friends, cracking open a textbook, or simply craving a warm cup on a rainy Assam afternoon — Coffee Country is your place. Every cup is made with care, every smile is genuine, and every visit feels like coming home.
